⚽ FC Barcelona - Més que un club

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is more than a football club - it is a symbol of passion, culture, and pride. From the artistry of Johan Cruyff to the golden era of Messi, Xavi, and Iniesta, Barça has shaped the beautiful game with its tiki-taka style and unforgettable triumphs. Wearing the iconic blaugrana colors, the club inspires millions through its legendary victories, the famed La Masia academy, and its motto: “Més que un club” — More than a club.
